"AIRIE"

Doctors stress release device

Project:

Philadelphia university offered a design factory product development project in which students from different fields( architecture, design, health care, computer science) were grouped together to design a product related to health science



Problem:

Using technology devlop an application or device which can help doctors or nurses reduce stress in their work

Solution:

Devlop a product which can interact with patients and store all information securly in database

Process:

Our team followed double diamond design thinking process to solve the given problem. Below image shows our process in detail



User Research:

Being that our challenge was related to doctors and nurses our team put a larger effort into studying the behavior of doctors and nurses and figuring out the heart of the problem. We interviewed some doctors on campus and read many online articles on stress in doctors life.




What we learned from User Research

We learned that doctors and nurses have to ask general set of questions to patients and write it down on a paper or feed in computer. Many a times doctors stress out because they find it difficult to pull out proper information from patients past records or from files, which leads to misdiagnosis. Improving communication between health providers can reduce patients died from preventable hospital mistakes. Doctors are also stressed because of high volume of patients they have to attend each day that to in a specified time.
Example a physician can spend only around 13 to 16 minutes with each patients



Features:

  • Airie is a device which interacts with patients and stores all its information securly into its database.
  • Airie has voice and facial recognition.
  • Hands free EMR data entry
  • Stores and transfers data within network
  • Coordinates patient care among providers
  • Soothing night light
  • Resets for each new patient


Next Steps:

Unfortunately due to limited time span and difficulty in working remotely we didn’t get to complete everything to our fullest desires. If given the opportunity to develop this product further, I would have liked to do more rounds of user-testing with our prototype and see how it tested in a real-world setting. From there, I would have further iterated and refined our product until we completed something that satisfied our end-users needs and accomplished the needs of our problem.

"Oyster Oasis"

Website with Data visualization and information on oysters

Organization :

Billion Oyster Project is an initiative of the New York Harbor Foundation, ecosystem restoration and education project aimed at restoring one billion live oysters to New York Harbor and engaging hundreds of thousands of school children through restoration based STEM education programs.


Problem:

Billion Oyster Project wants to make people aware about oysters and its extinction also they need a way for scientists to visualize their data of oysters.

Solution:

Develop a user friendly website which provides proper information about oysters also add data visualization that illustrates specific findings.



User Research:

We started our research by first understanding the core of the problem, we searched over internet and interviewed some of the workers and scientist available at New York harbor Governor’s Island about oysters and problems they face. We also asked New York people some basic question about oysters and tried to understand their level of knowledge about oysters.

What we learned from User Research

From our research we learned that scientists are definitely not experts in getting their data across visually. They needed to know the content of the information in a clean and simple manner and this was not at all the standard in the community.
We also learned that about 70% of people had below average information about oysters and its extinction, also many had false information about its extinction.





What is Oyster Oasis Mission:

The main goal of our website is to bring about higher awareness of the issues that are currently transpiring within the New York Harbor ecosystem and surrounding areas, especially where the population of oysters is concerned. This website also hopes to help spread the message that the program, Billion Oyster Project has already begun to communicate. The mission of the Billion Oyster Project, located on Governor's Island, is to take the first step towards repairing the damage inflicted on the oyster population within the New York Harbor by raising a population of 1 billion oysters within the harbor. Additionally, his website also seeks to provide a comprehensible format in which viewers can visualize the data associated with the Billion Oyster Project. It aims to supply and collect virtual forms that will gather information concerning the oysters grown on Governor's Island in New York City by the Billion Oyster Project.




Features of our website:

  • Oyster Oasis website raises awareness about oysters and its extinction
  • Spreads message of Billion Oyster Project.
  • Educate vistirs about Oysters and their lifecyle
  • Helps in Data visualization
  • Our website has oyster survival game which can be entertaining for children
  • User Friendly website
